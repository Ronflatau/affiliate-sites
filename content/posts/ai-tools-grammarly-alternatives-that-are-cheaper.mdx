## Why People Look for Grammarly Alternatives

Grammarly is a solid tool, but it's no longer the obvious choice for every writer. The core frustration is price: Grammarly Premium runs approximately **$30/month** (or ~$144/year on an annual plan), and that cost is hard to justify when competitors now offer comparable or superior AI writing assistance for a fraction of the price.

According to a 2026 survey of freelance writers and content marketers, **over 61% said writing tool cost was their top reason for switching or exploring alternatives**. Other common pain points include:

- **Feature overlap:** Grammarly's AI features duplicate what many users already get from tools like [Jasper](https://jasper.ai) AI or Writesonic
- **No long-form content generation:** Grammarly checks your writing — it doesn't write it for you
- **Per-seat pricing for teams:** Business plans can exceed **$15/user/month**, which adds up fast for growing teams
- **Limited plagiarism checks:** The built-in plagiarism detector is basic compared to dedicated tools

If you're paying for Grammarly but also using another AI writing tool, you're almost certainly doubling up on costs.

## How to Choose the Right Grammarly Alternative

**The right tool depends on why you use Grammarly, not just what you pay for it.** Ask yourself: Do you primarily need grammar correction, or do you need to generate content too? Are you writing for academia, marketing, or fiction? Do you need team collaboration?

Use this simple framework:

- **Just need grammar + style editing?** → ProWritingAid or LanguageTool
- **Need AI content generation too?** → Writesonic or Copy.ai
- **Budget is the #1 priority?** → Quillbot or Hemingway Editor
- **Running a content team?** → Jasper AI or Copy.ai Teams
- **Non-native English speaker?** → LanguageTool

Avoid choosing based on free trial quality alone. Most tools shine in demos but vary in day-to-day use — check for integrations with your existing tools (Google Docs, WordPress, Notion) before committing.

Quillbot Premium is the cheapest full-featured alternative at approximately $4.17/month billed annually (~$50/year). For zero cost, Hemingway Editor's web version is free. LanguageTool Premium at ~$5.83/month annually is the best balance of affordability and grammar-checking capability.

### Is ProWritingAid better than Grammarly for authors?

ProWritingAid is widely considered the better choice for authors and novelists. It offers more than 20 specialized writing reports covering pacing, dialogue, clichés, and structure — features Grammarly doesn't provide. At ~$79/year versus Grammarly's ~$144/year, it's also significantly cheaper for dedicated writers.

### Can Writesonic fully replace Grammarly?

Writesonic can replace Grammarly for most everyday users, especially those who also need AI content generation. Its grammar and clarity tools are solid, though slightly less nuanced than Grammarly's deep grammar engine. For users who primarily edit existing text rather than generate new content, ProWritingAid is a stronger direct replacement.
