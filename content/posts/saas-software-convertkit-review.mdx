## FAQ

### Is ConvertKit free?

ConvertKit offers a free plan up to 1,000 subscribers with basic features. Paid plans start at $25/month and include advanced automation, landing pages, and paid subscriptions.

### Can I export my subscribers if I leave ConvertKit?

Yes. ConvertKit allows you to export your subscriber list at any time, though some features like segmentation tags won't carry over to other platforms.

### Does ConvertKit integrate with WordPress?

ConvertKit has a WordPress plugin, but it's limited to forms and opt-ins. For deeper integration, you'll need to use their API or third-party tools like Zapier (though this can add complexity).
