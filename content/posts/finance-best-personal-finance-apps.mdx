## Key Features to Look For in 2026

### Security & Data Protection
Your financial information is sensitive. Look for apps using:
- 256-bit bank-level encryption
- Two-factor authentication
- Regular security audits
- FDIC insurance on cash holdings
- SOC 2 compliance

### Account Aggregation
The best apps connect to all your financial accounts—banks, credit cards, investment accounts, retirement plans. This gives you a complete financial picture in one dashboard.

### Automation Capabilities
Manual finances don't scale. Premium apps in 2026 offer:
- Automatic savings transfers
- Bill pay automation
- Recurring investments
- Debt payoff scheduling

### Investment Options
Ensure the app offers:
- Low-cost index funds and ETFs
- Diversification across asset classes
- Fractional share investing
- Alternative investments (real estate, crypto if desired)
